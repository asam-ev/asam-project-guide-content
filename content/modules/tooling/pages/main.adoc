TIP: Most roles can contribute by **{no-installation}**.

Tools on these pages are separated into mandatory infrastructure and recommended tools. +
<<Infrastructure For Standard Development,**Mandatory infrastructure**>> contains everything that is mandatory for every project.
These platforms and tools are often hosted by ASAM and always accessible online. +
<<Recommended Tools, **Recommended tools**>> are tools that in provide additional features beyond their online equivalent and can be beneficial for certain users.
ASAM provides helpful guides and tips here.

It also is a central aspect of ASAM's concept of xref:concepts:doc-as-code.adoc[].
