TIP: Most roles can contribute by **{no-installation}**.

Tools in the Project Guide are separated into mandatory infrastructure and recommended tools. +
**Mandatory infrastructure** contains everything that is mandatory for every project.
These platforms and tools are often hosted by ASAM and always accessible online.
Find out more about it in xref:working-without-software-installation.adoc[]. +
<<Recommended Tools, **Recommended tools**>> are tools that in provide additional features beyond their online equivalent and can be beneficial for certain users.
ASAM provides helpful guides and tips here.

It is also a central aspect of ASAM's concept of xref:concepts:doc-as-code.adoc[].
