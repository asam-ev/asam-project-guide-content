== Context
You are interested in a proposed idea and want to join in its discussions and possible the following Proposal phase?
Follow the steps below.


== Result
You have signed up for updates and events around the idea.

[#prerequisites-idea-submittal]
== Prerequisites
* You must be an ASAM member or be sponsored by one


== How

. Send an email to the address listed on the idea page
. The ASAM Office (GTM) will send you a confirmation

Also see https://www.asam.net/active-projects/ideation/how-it-works/[the ASAM website^] for more information.
